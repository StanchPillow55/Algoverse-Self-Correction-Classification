To determine how much more money Fern will make from the Widgeteer 3000 compared to the WidgetMaster 9000, we need to calculate the total revenue generated by each machine over the course of a month and then find the difference.

### Step 1: Calculate the total number of widgets produced by each machine in a month.

**Widgeteer 3000:**
- Widgets per hour: 60
- Hours per day: 24
- Days per month: 30

Total widgets produced by Widgeteer 3000 in a month:
\[ 
60 \, \text{widgets/hour} \times 24 \, \text{hours/day} \times 30 \, \text{days} = 43,200 \, \text{widgets} 
\]

**WidgetMaster 9000:**
- Widgets per hour: 45
- Hours per day: 24
- Days per month: 30

Total widgets produced by WidgetMaster 9000 in a month:
\[ 
45 \, \text{widgets/hour} \times 24 \, \text{hours/day} \times 30 \, \text{days} = 32,400 \, \text{widgets} 
\]

### Step 2: Calculate the total revenue from each machine.

**Revenue per widget:** $6

**Revenue from Widgeteer 3000:**
\[ 
43,200 \, \text{widgets} \times 6 \, \text{dollars/widget} = 259,200 \, \text{dollars} 
\]

**Revenue from WidgetMaster 9000:**
\[ 
32,400 \, \text{widgets} \times 6 \, \text{dollars/widget} = 194,400 \, \text{dollars} 
\]

### Step 3: Calculate the difference in revenue.

Difference in revenue:
\[ 
259,200 \, \text{dollars} - 194,400 \, \text{dollars} = 64,800 \, \text{dollars} 
\]

### Final Answer:
Fern will make $64,800 more from the Widgeteer 3000 compared to the WidgetMaster 9000 over the course of a month.
