Ann is 9 years old. Her brother is twice her age, which means her brother is 9 * 2 = 18 years old.

In 3 years, Ann's brother will be 18 + 3 = <<18+3=21>>21 years old.

So, Ann's brother will be 21 years old in 3 years.
