To determine how long James spends reading 18 pages of his book, we first need to find out how long it takes him to read a single page based on the information provided.

1. **Calculate the reading rate:**
   James reads 3 pages in 10 minutes. To find out how long it takes him to read one page, we can set up the following calculation:

   \[
   \text{Time per page} = \frac{\text{Total time}}{\text{Number of pages}} = \frac{10 \text{ minutes}}{3 \text{ pages}} 
   \]

   Performing the division:

   \[
   \text{Time per page} = \frac{10}{3} \text{ minutes per page} \approx 3.33 \text{ minutes per page}
   \]

2. **Calculate the total time for 18 pages:**
   Now that we know it takes James approximately \( \frac{10}{3} \) minutes to read one page, we can find out how long it takes him to read 18 pages:

   \[
   \text{Total time for 18 pages} = \text{Time per page} \times \text{Number of pages} = \left(\frac{10}{3} \text{ minutes per page}\right) \times 18 \text{ pages}
   \]

   Performing the multiplication:

   \[
   \text{Total time for 18 pages} = \frac{10 \times 18}{3} = \frac{180}{3} = 60 \text{ minutes}
   \]

3. **Final answer:**
   Therefore, the total time James spends reading 18 pages is:

   \[
   \boxed{60} \text{ minutes}
   \]
