  \[

  \]

**Step 4: Calculate the time taken to swim 3 miles on a hot day.**
- Since Ray swims 1 mile in 34 minutes, for 3 miles:
  \[
  3 \times 34 \text{ minutes} = 102 \text{ minutes}
  \]

**Step 5: Find the difference in time between swimming on a hot day and a cold day.**
- We subtract the time taken on a cold day from the time taken on a hot day:
  \[
  102 \text{ minutes} - 48 \text{ minutes} = 54 \text{ minutes}
  \]

Ray takes 54 minutes longer to swim 3 miles on a hot day than on a cold day.
