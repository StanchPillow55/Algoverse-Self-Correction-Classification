To find the total cost of the pastries that Toula bought, we will calculate the cost for each type of pastry separately and then sum them up.

1. **Cost of Donuts**:
   - Toula bought 3 dozen donuts.
   - The cost per dozen is $68.
   - Total cost for donuts = Number of dozens × Cost per dozen
   \[
   \text{Total cost for donuts} = 3 \text{ dozen} \times 68 \text{ dollars/dozen} = 204 \text{ dollars}
   \]

2. **Cost of Mini Cupcakes**:
   - Toula bought 2 dozen mini cupcakes.
   - The cost per dozen is $80.
   - Total cost for mini cupcakes = Number of dozens × Cost per dozen
   \[
   \text{Total cost for mini cupcakes} = 2 \text{ dozen} \times 80 \text{ dollars/dozen} = 160 \text{ dollars}
   \]

3. **Cost of Mini Cheesecakes**:
   - Toula bought 6 dozen mini cheesecakes.
   - The cost per dozen is $55.
   - Total cost for mini cheesecakes = Number of dozens × Cost per dozen
   \[
   \text{Total cost for mini cheesecakes} = 6 \text{ dozen} \times 55 \text{ dollars/dozen} = 330 \text{ dollars}
   \]

4. **Total Cost**:
   - Now, we will sum the total costs of all the pastries.
   \[
   \text{Total cost} = \text{Total cost for donuts} + \text{Total cost for mini cupcakes} + \text{Total cost for mini cheesecakes}
   \]
   \[
   \text{Total cost} = 204 \text{ dollars} + 160 \text{ dollars} + 330 \text{ dollars} = 694 \text{ dollars}
   \]

Thus, the total cost of all the pastries Toula bought is **$694**.
