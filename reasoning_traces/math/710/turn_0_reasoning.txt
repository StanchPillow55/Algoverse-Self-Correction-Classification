To find Marcus' total bill, we will calculate the cost of each type of item he ordered, sum those costs, and then apply the 10% discount from his loyalty card.

1. **Calculate the cost of the croissants:**
   - Number of croissants: 5
   - Price per croissant: $3.00
   - Total cost for croissants = Number of croissants × Price per croissant
   \[
   \text{Total cost for croissants} = 5 \times 3.00 = 15.00
   \]

2. **Calculate the cost of the cinnamon rolls:**
   - Number of cinnamon rolls: 4
   - Price per cinnamon roll: $2.50
   - Total cost for cinnamon rolls = Number of cinnamon rolls × Price per cinnamon roll
   \[
   \text{Total cost for cinnamon rolls} = 4 \times 2.50 = 10.00
   \]

3. **Calculate the cost of the mini quiches:**
   - Number of mini quiches: 3
   - Price per mini quiche: $4.00
   - Total cost for mini quiches = Number of mini quiches × Price per mini quiche
   \[
   \text{Total cost for mini quiches} = 3 \times 4.00 = 12.00
   \]

4. **Calculate the cost of the blueberry muffins:**
   - Number of blueberry muffins: 13
   - Price per blueberry muffin: $1.00
   - Total cost for blueberry muffins = Number of blueberry muffins × Price per blueberry muffin
   \[
   \text{Total cost for blueberry muffins} = 13 \times 1.00 = 13.00
   \]

5. **Sum the total costs:**
   - Total cost = Total cost for croissants + Total cost for cinnamon rolls + Total cost for mini quiches + Total cost for blueberry muffins
   \[
   \text{Total cost} = 15.00 + 10.00 + 12.00 + 13.00
   \]
   \[
   \text{Total cost} = 50.00
   \]

6. **Apply the 10% discount:**
   - Discount amount = Total cost × Discount rate
   \[
   \text{Discount amount} = 50.00 \times 0.10 = 5.00
   \]
   - Total after discount = Total cost - Discount amount
   \[
   \text{Total after discount} = 50.00 - 5.00 = 45.00
   \]

Thus, Marcus' total bill after applying the 10% discount is \(\boxed{45.00}\).
