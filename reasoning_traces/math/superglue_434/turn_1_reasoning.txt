Calculate the sum of all even numbers between 1 and 100.

2. **Determine the Sequence:**

   - The first term \( a_1 = 2 \)
   - The common difference \( d = 2 \)
   - The last term \( a_n = 100 \)

3. **Find the Number of Terms:**
   To find the number of terms \( n \) in the sequence, use the formula for the nth term of an arithmetic sequence:
   \[
   a_n = a_1 + (n-1) \cdot d
   \]

   \[
   100 = 2 + (n-1) \cdot 2
   \]
   \[
   100 = 2 + 2n - 2

   \[
   100 = 2n
   \]
   \[
   n = \frac{100}{2} = 50
   \]

4. **Calculate the Sum of the Sequence:**
   The sum \( S_n \) of an arithmetic sequence is given by:
   \[
   S_n = \frac{n}{2} \cdot (a_1 + a_n)
   \]
   Substituting the known values:
   \[
   S_{50} = \frac{50}{2} \cdot (2 + 100)
   \]
   \[
   S_{50} = 25 \cdot 102
   \]
   \[
   S_{50} = 2550
   \]

   The sum of all even numbers between 1 and 100 is 2550.
