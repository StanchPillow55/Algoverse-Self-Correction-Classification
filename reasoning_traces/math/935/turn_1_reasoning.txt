To determine how much more money Fern will make from the Widgeteer 3000 compared to the WidgetMaster 9000, we will follow these steps:

1. **Calculate the total number of widgets produced by each machine in a month.**
2. **Calculate the total revenue generated by each machine.**
3. **Find the difference in revenue between the two machines.**

### Step 1: Calculate the total number of widgets produced

**For the Widgeteer 3000:**
- Production rate: 60 widgets per hour
- Hours per day: 24
- Days in a month: 30

Total widgets produced by Widgeteer 3000 in a month:
\[
\text{Total widgets} = \text{Production rate} \times \text{Hours per day} \times \text{Days in a month}
\]
\[
\text{Total widgets} = 60 \, \text{widgets/hour} \times 24 \, \text{hours/day} \times 30 \, \text{days}
\]
\[
\text{Total widgets} = 60 \times 24 \times 30 = 43,200 \, \text{widgets}
\]

**For the WidgetMaster 9000:**
- Production rate: 45 widgets per hour

Total widgets produced by WidgetMaster 9000 in a month:
\[
\text{Total widgets} = 45 \, \text{widgets/hour} \times 24 \, \text{hours/day} \times 30 \, \text{days}
\]
\[
\text{Total widgets} = 45 \times 24 \times 30 = 32,400 \, \text{widgets}
\]

### Step 2: Calculate the total revenue generated by each machine

**Revenue per widget:** $6

**For the Widgeteer 3000:**
\[
\text{Total revenue} = \text{Total widgets} \times \text{Price per widget}
\]
\[
\text{Total revenue} = 43,200 \, \text{widgets} \times 6 \, \text{dollars/widget} = 259,200 \, \text{dollars}
\]

**For the WidgetMaster 9000:**
\[
\text{Total revenue} = 32,400 \, \text{widgets} \times 6 \, \text{dollars/widget} = 194,400 \, \text{dollars}
\]

### Step 3: Find the difference in revenue

Now, we calculate how much more money Fern will make from the Widgeteer 3000 compared to the WidgetMaster 9000:
\[
\text{Difference in revenue} = \text{Revenue from Widgeteer 3000} - \text{Revenue from WidgetMaster 9000}
\]
\[
\text{Difference in revenue} = 259,200 \, \text{dollars} - 194,400 \, \text{dollars} = 64,800 \, \text{dollars}
\]

### Final Answer
Fern will make **$64,800** more from the Widgeteer 3000 compared to the WidgetMaster 9000 if it runs 24 hours a day for a month with 30 days.
