   - Since there are 4 quarts in a gallon, we calculate the total quarts:
     \[

     \]

2. **Determine the number of bottles of champagne needed**:
   - Each bottle of champagne holds 1 quart.
   - Therefore, Jackson needs 160 bottles of champagne to fill the hot tub.

   - Each bottle costs $50.
   - The total cost for 160 bottles is:
     \[
     160 \text{ bottles} \times 50 \text{ dollars/bottle} = 8000 \text{ dollars}
     \]

4. **Apply the 20% volume discount**:
   - A 20% discount means Jackson pays 80% of the total cost.
   - To find the discounted price, we calculate:
     \[
     8000 \text{ dollars} \times (1 - 0.20) = 8000 \text{ dollars} \times 0.80 = 6400 \text{ dollars}
     \]

5. **Final answer**:
   - Therefore, the total amount Jackson spends on champagne is:
     \[
     \boxed{6400 \text{ dollars}}
