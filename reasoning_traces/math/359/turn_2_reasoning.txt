To solve this problem, we need to determine how many pages each person reads in 240 minutes and then sum these amounts to find the total number of pages read by all three individuals.

### Step-by-Step Solution:

1. **Determine the reading rate for each person:**

   - **Rene's reading rate:**
     Rene reads 30 pages in 60 minutes. Therefore, his reading rate is:
     \[
     \text{Rene's rate} = \frac{30 \text{ pages}}{60 \text{ minutes}} = \frac{1 \text{ page}}{2 \text{ minutes}}
     \]

   - **Lulu's reading rate:**
     Lulu reads 27 pages in 60 minutes. Therefore, her reading rate is:
     \[
     \text{Lulu's rate} = \frac{27 \text{ pages}}{60 \text{ minutes}} = \frac{9 \text{ pages}}{20 \text{ minutes}}
     \]

   - **Cherry's reading rate:**
     Cherry reads 25 pages in 60 minutes. Therefore, her reading rate is:
     \[
     \text{Cherry's rate} = \frac{25 \text{ pages}}{60 \text{ minutes}} = \frac{5 \text{ pages}}{12 \text{ minutes}}
     \]

2. **Calculate the number of pages each person reads in 240 minutes:**

   - **Rene's pages in 240 minutes:**
     Using Rene's rate, we calculate the number of pages he reads in 240 minutes:
     \[
     \text{Rene's pages} = \left(\frac{1 \text{ page}}{2 \text{ minutes}}\right) \times 240 \text{ minutes} = 120 \text{ pages}
     \]

   - **Lulu's pages in 240 minutes:**
     Using Lulu's rate, we calculate the number of pages she reads in 240 minutes:
     \[
     \text{Lulu's pages} = \left(\frac{9 \text{ pages}}{20 \text{ minutes}}\right) \times 240 \text{ minutes} = \frac{9 \times 240}{20} = 108 \text{ pages}
     \]

   - **Cherry's pages in 240 minutes:**
     Using Cherry's rate, we calculate the number of pages she reads in 240 minutes:
     \[
     \text{Cherry's pages} = \left(\frac{5 \text{ pages}}{12 \text{ minutes}}\right) \times 240 \text{ minutes} = \frac{5 \times 240}{12} = 100 \text{ pages}
     \]

3. **Calculate the total number of pages read by all three:**

   Add the pages read by Rene, Lulu, and Cherry:
   \[
   \text{Total pages} = 120 \text{ pages} + 108 \text{ pages} + 100 \text{ pages} = 328 \text{ pages}
   \]

### Final Answer:

The total number of pages read by Rene, Lulu, and Cherry in 240 minutes is **328 pages**.
