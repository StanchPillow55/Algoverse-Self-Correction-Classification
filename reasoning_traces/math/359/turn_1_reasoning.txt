To solve this problem, we need to determine how many pages each person has read in 240 minutes and then sum these amounts to find the total number of pages read by all three individuals.

### Step-by-Step Solution:

1. **Determine the reading rate for each person:**

   - **Rene's reading rate:**
     Rene can read 30 pages in 60 minutes. Therefore, Rene's reading rate is:
     \[
     \text{Rene's rate} = \frac{30 \text{ pages}}{60 \text{ minutes}} = \frac{1 \text{ page}}{2 \text{ minutes}}
     \]

   - **Lulu's reading rate:**
     Lulu can read 27 pages in 60 minutes. Therefore, Lulu's reading rate is:
     \[
     \text{Lulu's rate} = \frac{27 \text{ pages}}{60 \text{ minutes}} = \frac{9 \text{ pages}}{20 \text{ minutes}}
     \]

   - **Cherry's reading rate:**
     Cherry can read 25 pages in 60 minutes. Therefore, Cherry's reading rate is:
     \[
     \text{Cherry's rate} = \frac{25 \text{ pages}}{60 \text{ minutes}} = \frac{5 \text{ pages}}{12 \text{ minutes}}
     \]

2. **Calculate the number of pages each person reads in 240 minutes:**

   - **Pages read by Rene in 240 minutes:**
     \[
     \text{Pages by Rene} = \left(\frac{1 \text{ page}}{2 \text{ minutes}}\right) \times 240 \text{ minutes} = 120 \text{ pages}
     \]

   - **Pages read by Lulu in 240 minutes:**
     \[
     \text{Pages by Lulu} = \left(\frac{9 \text{ pages}}{20 \text{ minutes}}\right) \times 240 \text{ minutes} = \left(\frac{9 \times 240}{20}\right) \text{ pages} = 108 \text{ pages}
     \]

   - **Pages read by Cherry in 240 minutes:**
     \[
     \text{Pages by Cherry} = \left(\frac{5 \text{ pages}}{12 \text{ minutes}}\right) \times 240 \text{ minutes} = \left(\frac{5 \times 240}{12}\right) \text{ pages} = 100 \text{ pages}
     \]

3. **Calculate the total number of pages read by all three:**

   \[
   \text{Total pages} = \text{Pages by Rene} + \text{Pages by Lulu} + \text{Pages by Cherry} = 120 + 108 + 100 = 328 \text{ pages}
   \]

### Final Answer:
The total number of pages read by Rene, Lulu, and Cherry in 240 minutes is 328 pages.
