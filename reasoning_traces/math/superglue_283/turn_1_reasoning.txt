### Sample Problem:
**Question:** A farmer has a rectangular field that is 100 meters long and 60 meters wide. He wants to divide the field into two equal parts by constructing a fence parallel to one of the sides. What is the length of the fence needed?

### Step-by-Step Solution:

1. **Understand the Problem:**
   - We have a rectangular field with dimensions 100 meters (length) and 60 meters (width).
   - The field needs to be divided into two equal parts using a fence parallel to one of the sides.

2. **Determine the Orientation of the Fence:**
   - The fence can be parallel to either the length (100 meters) or the width (60 meters) of the rectangle.
   - Dividing the field into two equal parts means each part will have half the area of the original field.

3. **Calculate the Area of the Field:**
   - The area of the rectangle is given by the formula:
     \[
     \text{Area} = \text{Length} \times \text{Width} = 100 \, \text{m} \times 60 \, \text{m} = 6000 \, \text{m}^2
     \]

4. **Divide the Field into Two Equal Parts:**
   - Each part should have an area of:
     \[
     \frac{6000 \, \text{m}^2}{2} = 3000 \, \text{m}^2
     \]

5. **Determine the Length of the Fence:**
   - If the fence is parallel to the length (100 meters), it will divide the width (60 meters) into two equal parts.
   - Each part will have a width of:
     \[
     \frac{60 \, \text{m}}{2} = 30 \, \text{m}
     \]
   - Therefore, the length of the fence needed is 100 meters.

   - Alternatively, if the fence is parallel to the width (60 meters), it will divide the length (100 meters) into two equal parts.
   - Each part will have a length of:
     \[
     \frac{100 \, \text{m}}{2} = 50 \, \text{m}
     \]
   - Therefore, the length of the fence needed is 60 meters.

6. **Conclusion:**
   - The farmer can choose to place the fence either parallel to the length or the width.
   - The length of the fence needed will be either 100 meters or 60 meters, depending on the orientation chosen.

### Final Answer:
The length of the fence needed is either 100 meters or 60 meters, depending on whether the fence is parallel to the width or the length of the field, respectively.
