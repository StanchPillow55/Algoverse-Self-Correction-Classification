To convert Ashley's internet connection speed from kilobits per second (kb/s) to megabits per hour (Mb/h), we will follow these steps:

1. **Convert kilobits to megabits**: 
   We know that 1 megabit (Mb) is equal to 1000 kilobits (kb). Therefore, to convert kilobits to megabits, we can use the formula:
   \[
   \text{Megabits} = \frac{\text{Kilobits}}{1000}
   \]

2. **Convert seconds to hours**: 
   There are 3600 seconds in one hour. Therefore, to find out how many kilobits are transferred in one hour, we multiply the speed in kilobits per second by the number of seconds in an hour:
   \[
   \text{Kilobits per hour} = \text{Kilobits per second} \times 3600
   \]

3. **Calculate Ashley's speed in kilobits per hour**:
   Given that Ashley's internet speed is 20 kb/s, we can calculate her speed in kilobits per hour:
   \[
   \text{Kilobits per hour} = 20 \, \text{kb/s} \times 3600 \, \text{s/h}
   \]
   \[
   \text{Kilobits per hour} = 20 \times 3600 = 72000 \, \text{kb/h}
   \]

4. **Convert kilobits per hour to megabits per hour**:
   Now we will convert the kilobits per hour to megabits per hour using the conversion factor we established earlier:
   \[
   \text{Megabits per hour} = \frac{\text{Kilobits per hour}}{1000}
   \]
   \[
   \text{Megabits per hour} = \frac{72000 \, \text{kb/h}}{1000} = 72 \, \text{Mb/h}
   \]

Thus, Ashley's internet connection speed is **72 megabits per hour**.

Final answer: **72 Mb/h**.
